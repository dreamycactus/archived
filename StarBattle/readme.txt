Partial game engine
Coded in summer of 2011

Features:
SAT Polygon collision detection adapted from a tutorial of Chris hecker's tutorial on Rigid Body Dynamics and codeproject SAT tutorial
Rigid Body Dynamics adpated from a tutorial of Chris Hecker's tutorial on Rigid Body Dynamics
Sweep and Prune (Actually box prune) adapted from Pierre Terdiman's Opcode library. So good! He's my hero.

There may be remnants of a quad tree implementation in there somewhere too.
